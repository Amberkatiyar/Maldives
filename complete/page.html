The Maldives officially the Republic of Maldives, is a small island nation in South Asia, situated in the Arabian Sea of the Indian Ocean. It lies southwest of Sri Lanka and India, about 700 kilometres from the Asian continent's mainland.The Maldives is one of the world's most geographically dispersed sovereign states as well as the smallest Asian country by land area and population, with around 515,696 inhabitants.

maldives Food

Here are the Popular food of maldives.One thing that we can assure of is that these delicious delicacies and refreshing beverages of Maldives will leave your stomach full and mood happy.

Masroshi
Masroshi is a chapati, stuffed with smoked tuna and coconut. The added flavour of curry leaves and spices will make it more delicious.
Maldivian Lobster
Lobster tastes best when cooked alive it melts in the mouth and leaves a unique taste.
Fish Curry
Majority of popular food in Maldives include fish, so how can you not try the very basic and very important Fish curry - creamy, flavoured and spicy.
Rihaakuru
A traditional food of Maldivian cuisine, Rihaakuru is a thick paste made by cooking fish for a very long time.


Weather

Temperatures in the Maldives stay at a wonderfully warm 25-30°Cyear-round and only drop a few degrees at night. There’s an average of 8 hours of sunshine every day and the water temperature barely falls below 25°C.
Maldives is between December and April when the climate is drier, it’s less windy and the weather is hotter.
